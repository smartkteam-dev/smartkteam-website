The kitchen

Cooking that
doesn't apologize

Executive Chef Mei Nakamura grew up between Tokyo, Seoul, and Manila — eating street food at 6am and Michelin-starred tasting menus at midnight. HANABI is the restaurant she always wanted to eat at but never found: bold Pan-Asian cooking without the pretension, in a room that feels like an old friend's house.

Every dish on the menu is the result of obsession — sourced from local California farms, inspired by flavors across 12 countries, and executed with the precision of someone who has absolutely nothing to prove.
