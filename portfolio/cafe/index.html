Our story · Since 2019

"We didn't open a coffee shop. We opened a place to actually get things done."

Slowpour started when our founder Maya Lin couldn't find a cafe in the Mission that had both genuinely good coffee and a reliable internet connection. So she built one herself — with input from 60 regulars who filled out a Google Form about what they actually needed in a workspace.

Five years later, we've hosted over 400 events, launched the SF Slowpour Community Fund, and still greet most of our morning regulars by name.

"The best places to work aren't offices. They're the places that feel like they were made for you."
— Maya Lin, Founder
